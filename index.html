Research & Development

As a former freelance developer, I used to work on many PHP projects, I am mastering the framework Symfony 3 & 4. As a part of my personal R&D, I started learning node.js using Express and Adonis. Since the beginning of my scholarship at HETIC, I learned a lot about technical architecture, workflow environment and security. The hard part of programming is all about solving problems and secure the whole architecture, and no one should neglect this part in favor of marketing.

2018 is a rich year in research and development for me, I discover technologies like React, Vue and node.js thanks to projects like Dreamvo or PeerTube. In the end of 2017, I created Dreamvo, a decentralized video sharing platform based on WebTorrent and PeerTube. The main goal is to create an alternative to YouTube that offers another vision of sharing, viewing and managing our content on the Internet. This project require a really good thought about marketing, architecture and code.
